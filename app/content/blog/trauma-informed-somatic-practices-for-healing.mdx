Somatic approaches to trauma resolution acknowledge the fundamental truth that traumatic experiences are not merely psychological events but are deeply encoded in our physiological systems. The body keeps the score, maintaining patterns of tension, constriction, and dysregulation that persist long after the traumatic event has passed. Through mindful movement, breathwork, and interoceptive awareness practices, we can gently access and release these embodied trauma patterns, restoring the nervous system's natural capacity for regulation and resilience.
